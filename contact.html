What to Include in Your Enquiry

To help us understand your requirements and provide an accurate response, please include the following information where possible:

Project Details

  • Project type (new build, refurbishment, etc.)
  • Building use (residential, commercial, etc.)
  • Project location
  • Approximate size or scale
  • Current project stage

Service Requirements

  • Required disciplines (HVAC, electrical, etc.)
  • Design stage needed
  • Any specific requirements
  • Sustainability targets (BREEAM, EPC, etc.)
  • Any constraints or challenges

Timeline & Budget

  • Expected project start date
  • Required completion date
  • Budget considerations
  • Any urgent requirements
  • Preferred contact method
